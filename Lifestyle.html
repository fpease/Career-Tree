Lifestyle

Entry Level Career: Database Analyst

Database analysts dig into big data, meet with the analytics team, analyze data systems, and prepare reports. They make a good enough salary to be able to live comfortably. There is not too much room to buy really fancy things but you make enough money to get the things that are necessary to live comfortably. Some experiences you get with being a data analyst is you become good in sales, marketing, finance, data assurance and business intelligence.


Technical Career: Software/Game Development

Software/game developers spend most of their time planning and creating the game and the art for the game. They also will spend time debugging and writing out their code. They have a very good salary. So, they can live very comfortably with a little room to buy nicer things or go on nicer vacations.





Professional Career: Cyber Security Analyst

A cyber security analyst protects their companies hardware, software and networks from cyber criminals. They need to understand their company's IT infrastructure and need to monitor it at all times. Cyber security analysts have a very good salary. It is enough to live very comfortably with some room to go on a nice vacation or to buy nice things. Some experiences that you will get would be things like installing firewalls, encryption tools, and reporting breaches or weak spots.
